🚀 Getting Started
First 5 minutes
1

Set up your profile

Go to Settings → Profile. Add your name, date of birth, goals, and a short "about you". The AI uses this to personalize every response.

2

Add a free Groq API key

Go to console.groq.com → sign up free → create API key → paste in Settings → Integrations. This unlocks all AI features for free.

3

Add your first task

Go to Tasks → type something you need to do today → press Enter. That's it.

4

Talk to your AI

Go to Intelligence → type anything. Ask for help, plan your day, vent, learn something. The AI knows your tasks and habits and responds personally.

💡 Tip

The more you fill in your profile (goals, about you), the smarter and more personal the AI becomes. Spend 2 minutes on it.

Flashcards (Learner mode only)

1

Switch to Learner mode

Click the 🎓 Learner tab at the top of the AI screen.

2

Ask AI to teach you something

"Teach me about SQL injection" or "Explain how HTTPS works".

3

Say "yes" when AI offers a flashcard

AI will ask "Want me to create a flashcard?" — say yes and it's saved.

4

Review cards anytime

Click the "Cards" button in Learner mode, or go to Dashboard to see your flashcards.

✅ Tasks
Get things done
+

Add a task

Type in the input box and press Enter or the + button. Tasks are organized by date automatically.

✓

Complete a task

Tap the circle next to any task to mark it done. Completed tasks move to the Practice queue so you can be examined on what you learned.

🗑

Delete a task

Swipe or tap the delete icon on any task.

💡 Pro tip

You can ask the AI to manage your tasks: "Add a task: review cybersecurity notes" or "Delete my completed tasks". The AI does it silently in the background.

🔥 Habits
Build consistency

Habits are things you want to do every day (or certain days). The app tracks your streaks and shows a weekly calendar view.

1

Add a habit

Go to Habits (Daily Rituals) → type the habit name → press +. Examples: "Workout", "No junk food", "Study English 30 min".

2

Log it daily

Tap the habit card each day when you complete it. It turns green and builds your streak.

3

Track streaks

Dashboard shows your longest streaks and consistency percentage. Try to keep the chain unbroken.

💡 AI tip

Say to AI: "Mark my workout habit as done" or "Add a new habit: read 20 pages". AI handles it for you.

🎓 Practice Core
Exam + Quiz — test your knowledge

Practice Core has two modes: Exam (AI examines you on completed tasks) and Quiz (click-to-answer multiple choice on any topic).

📝 Exam Mode

1

Complete a task first

Go to Tasks → tick a task as done. It moves to the Practice queue.

2

Open Practice Core

Click the task from the left sidebar. AI starts the exam immediately.

3

Answer the questions

AI asks 5 questions (mix of multiple choice and written). Type your answers naturally.

4

Get graded

AI grades you out of 100, gives feedback, and saves the result to your History tab permanently.

🎯 Quiz Mode

1

Click 🎯 Quiz tab

Type any topic OR tap one of the quick suggestion buttons (Cybersecurity, IELTS, Linux, etc.).

2

Choose question count

Pick 5, 10, 15, or 20 questions. Click "Generate Quiz".

3

Click A, B, C, D

Tap the answer button. Green = correct, Red = wrong. Each wrong answer shows an explanation.

4

Click "Check Results"

After answering all questions — see your total score and all explanations.

💡 Results tab

All exam results are saved in the 📊 Results tab permanently. Expand any result to re-read the full exam conversation. Delete when you're done reviewing.

📊 Dashboard & Themes
Your daily overview

The dashboard shows your stats, tasks, habits, and flashcards at a glance. It has 3 themes:

🌌

Glass Command

Dark, futuristic, grid-styled. Great for night use. Shows charts and analytics prominently.

⚡

Sport Energy

High contrast, energetic. Shows score, tasks, habits, flashcards and AI widget. Best for motivation.

🌿

Minimal Zen

Clean, calm, minimal. No clutter. Great if you want focus without distraction.

Change theme

Go to Settings → Appearance → Dashboard Theme. Pick your favourite.

The wall clock shows the real time in 12-hour format with an analog clock face. The date updates automatically.

🤖 AI Models Guide
Which model to use when

Auto mode is recommended for most people — it picks the best available model and switches automatically if one runs out of tokens.

Model Best For Cost
✨ Auto Everything — smart fallback Free
Llama 3.3 70B Best quality free model. Daily use. Free
Llama 4 Scout PDFs, images, latest knowledge Free
DeepSeek R1 70B Complex reasoning, math, logic Free
GPT OSS 120B Most powerful free model Free
Qwen 3 32B Multilingual, reasoning tasks Free
Llama 3.1 8B Super fast replies, simple tasks Free
Gemini 2.5 Pro Smartest overall, long documents Needs Key
Gemini 2.5 Flash Fast + smart, great for daily use Needs Key
⚠️ Token limits

Free Groq models have daily token limits. If you hit the limit, switch to a different Groq model or use Auto mode which switches automatically. Limits reset daily.
